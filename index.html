Since 2025

Untuk Adifa, adek gua terbau sejagat Nottingham. Jujur, yang lain gue jelasin kenapa gue pilih karakter itu buat mereka. Lah lu doang yang milih sendiri. Pick me sumpah. Tapi ya, makasih juga udah selalu ada, bahkan pas gue ganggu tengah malam cuma buat galau karena putus dan segala dramanya. Lu emang keren sih. But that’s exactly what makes me feel lucky to have you and Alex as my Adek. Even though I tease you a lot, you remain the kind and wholeheartedly Adifa. Gausah nangis bacanya. Please don’t disappear, I still need someone to laugh my ass off with in the middle of the night. I hope everything goes smoothly for you, whether it’s school or friendship, and that whatever you’re chasing becomes easier to achieve. Remember, you are worth much more than what some people think you are. So please, never be taken for granted again. I’m wishing you continued success, InsyaAllah, until everything you’ve been dreaming of actually comes true. Makasih banyak ya, Dek.
